Innovation: Using Wind and Solar Energy for Electrical Supply

First Design

In our first design, we brainstormed and decide twin turbine blades connected by a braking system. However, we noticed an issue where the wind blades didn't function as intended.

Second Design

In our second design, we implemented interesting modifications to the wind turbine blades. We decided to add cones to the design to address the problem where the curved area of the blades didn't receive enough wind power when the wind blew to the west.
