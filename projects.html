PROCESS OF OUR INNOVATION PROJECT



Our primary focus
in the Innovation Project
mostly deals
with addressing the issue of
inadequate access to electricity in Okigwe.
Initially, we identified the problem:
lack of sufficient electricity in rural areas
We researched on the problem
we identified on okigwe's weather
analysis to check if it
can fit a project that
uses solar and wind
energy sources

In our brainstorming process we
came together as a team
to brainstorm designs using solar
and wind energy sources.
we also sketched out brainstormed
designs out .

We built out the designs
sketched in our brainstorming .
we made use of bolts
and screws, iron stands etc
to build our design .

When we tested our design
it made alot of mistakes
but we made changes to
them until it reached our
expectation
Our design includes gears,
iron stands , DC(Direct current)
generator , Battery pack and wires

were able to learn
how to Build our
robot designs and

Our brainstorming process
